# Prosopon Product Brief

## Purpose

This file is the source of truth for future website, copy, and design work on Prosopon. It helps Codex and other agents understand the company before making changes, so they do not rely only on page code, generated mockups, or incomplete design assets.

Use this file when changing:

- Website positioning, messaging, and page copy
- Service descriptions and case-study framing
- Visual design decisions and interaction tone
- Calls to action, navigation, and page structure
- SEO metadata and social preview language

## Register

Brand.

Prosopon's website is a professional services and consulting brand surface. Design is part of the product experience, but the site must primarily build credibility, clarity, and trust with senior decision-makers.

## Company

Prosopon Inc. is a Canadian federally and provincially incorporated professional services firm established in August 2021. It provides advisory, project delivery, and digital enablement services to organizations navigating complex transformation initiatives.

Prosopon supports work that requires structured execution, cross-functional coordination, disciplined delivery governance, and technology-informed decision-making.

## Mission

Prosopon enables organizations to deliver transformation initiatives with confidence by combining structured project delivery, sector-informed advisory services, and practical technology enablement that drives measurable business outcomes.

## Audience

Primary audiences:

- Senior executives and C-suite leaders
- Transformation, operations, and technology leaders
- Public sector and enterprise program owners
- Product, delivery, and portfolio leaders
- Organizations preparing for AI-informed transformation and modernization

Key sectors:

- Financial services
- Telecommunications
- Public sector
- Retail
- Other regulated or operationally complex organizations undergoing digital transformation

## Client Needs

Prosopon's clients typically need:

- Structured project execution in complex environments
- Sector-aware advisory, not generic consulting frameworks
- Delivery leadership that can coordinate cross-functional teams
- Practical technology enablement tied to business outcomes
- Governance for transformation programs
- AI-informed guidance that is credible, scoped, and industry-specific

## Services

Core service areas:

- Project, agile, and enterprise delivery management
- Digital enablement and operational process improvement
- Enterprise and digital transformation support
- Workflow automation and technology implementation
- Advisory services for modernization, execution readiness, and transformation governance
- AI-informed advisory for operations, customer engagement, analytics, and enterprise delivery

## Differentiators

Prosopon differentiates through:

- Proven delivery excellence in regulated and complex environments
- Credibility with senior leadership and decision-makers
- A structured, execution-focused approach to transformation
- Advisory work grounded in practical delivery experience
- Ability to tailor solutions to industry-specific requirements
- Flexible delivery model using senior oversight and specialist support where needed

- Rogers Communications
- TJX Canada
- Government of Alberta

Representative engagements:

- Government of Alberta grant application and financial management portal

## Tone

The tone should be:

- Credible
- Senior
- Clear
- Disciplined
- Practical
- Calm
- Sector-aware
- Outcome-focused

Avoid:

- Hype-heavy AI language
- Startup growth jargon
- Generic consulting platitudes
- Overpromising transformation outcomes
- Treating Prosopon like a software product company
- Fake metrics or invented claims
- Content that implies a large firm if the delivery model is intentionally flexible and specialist-led

## Design Direction

The design should feel like a premium professional services firm with modern heritage: authoritative, intellectual, restrained, and confident.

Use the `design_system` folder as the visual reference for:

- Logo
- Final color palette
- Consulting Noir design direction
- Dark premium surface treatment
- Antique gold accent usage
- Editorial spacing and restrained component styling

Use the current website and `docs` folder as the content source of truth.

Do not use generated design-system page copy directly unless it has been rewritten to match Prosopon's actual services, clients, and market.

## Website Goals

The website should:

- Establish trust quickly with senior enterprise and public sector buyers
- Explain what Prosopon does in plain language
- Show sector credibility through partners and case-study summaries
- Make the company feel focused, senior, and execution-oriented
- Support increased digital visibility as Prosopon invests in website upgrades and marketing
- Create a clear path to contact or schedule an introductory conversation

## Content Priorities

Prioritize:

- Transformation delivery and advisory
- Regulated and complex delivery environments
- Financial services, telecom, public sector, and retail experience
- Structured execution and delivery governance
- AI-informed advisory that is practical and industry-specific
- Case studies with concrete delivery context, not vague success stories

## Constraints

- Keep claims defensible from the provided docs and existing site.
- Clean encoding artifacts before using text from source documents.
- Preserve actual Prosopon content over placeholder generated content.
- Prefer concise executive-ready copy over long resume-style project descriptions.
- Do not add a backend, CMS, analytics, or live form submission unless explicitly requested.
